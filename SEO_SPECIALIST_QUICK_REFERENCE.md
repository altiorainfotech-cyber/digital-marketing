## Common Questions

**Q: Why do I need to select platforms?**
A: Platform selection helps track where assets are used and provides analytics to content creators.

**Q: Can I select multiple platforms?**
A: Yes! Select as many platforms as you need. The asset will be tagged with all selected platforms.

**Q: Does the download start immediately?**
A: Yes! After clicking "Download Now", the download starts automatically without opening a preview page.

**Q: Can I filter by multiple companies?**
A: Currently, you can filter by one company at a time. Use search to find specific assets across companies.

**Q: Where can I see my download history?**
A: Click "Download History" in the sidebar navigation.
